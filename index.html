My Story

Hello, Thanks for visiting my page. My name is Sohail. I was born in Mandalay which is a former royal capital of Myanmar. I am currently living in Malaysia. My story begins back in 2019 when I started teaching myself HTML and CSS using the FreeCodeCamp and SoloLearn app on my phone. The reason I started learning HTML and CSS is that I wanted to make web-app, web-design and web-animation. I coded 10 hours or more every day for the second website After a bit of practicing, I was able to make a website that looks okay. In 2020-2021 worked as a part time waiter at coffee shop (Koffie Craft- Dutch Restaurant) for side income while taking on freelance web projects. Currently i am working as a junior frontend developer under Epic Startup tech company.
